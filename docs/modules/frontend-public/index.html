frontend-public

Public customer-facing experience for content browsing, credit simulation, form submission, and multilingual information pages.

Technical foundation

  • Framework: React 18 + Vite SPA with route-based pages.
  • Routing: react-router-dom routes defined in frontend/src/App.jsx.
  • Localization: i18next with en, es, and pt strings shared across pages.
  • Backend integration through service clients in frontend/src/services, with demo fallbacks for static preview deployments.

Página de Informaciones Legales — /legal (SAIAPP3-25)

LegalPage.jsx provides a centralized, tabbed legal information hub accessible at /legal. It groups Legal Notice, Privacy Policy, and Terms & Conditions under a single accessible interface, replacing the previous scattered links from the footer.

  • Component: frontend/src/pages/LegalPage.jsx — inline tab state managed with React useState.
  • Accessibility: role="tablist", role="tab", role="tabpanel", aria-selected, aria-controls, aria-labelledby. Keyboard-accessible and screen-reader compatible.
  • i18n keys: legalPage.title, legalPage.subtitle, legalPage.tabLegalNotice, legalPage.tabPrivacy, legalPage.tabTerms, legalPage.ln.* (4 Legal Notice sections). Defined in es.json, en.json, and pt.json.
  • Header: new key header.legal added to all locales. Header.jsx includes the link both in desktop and mobile menus.
  • Footer: footer.legalNotice link updated from /terms to /legal.
  • Privacy and Terms tab content reuses existing privacyPage.* and termsPage.* i18n keys — zero duplication.
  • Legal Notice content covers: site owner, acceptable use, intellectual property, and limitation of liability.
  • Responsiveness: Tailwind classes ensure tabs stack on mobile and are fully reachable with min-height 44px touch targets.
